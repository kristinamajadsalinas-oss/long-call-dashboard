# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - Long Call Update Assistant

## ⚡ How It Works (Simple 3-Step Process)

### **Step 1: Configure Settings (One-Time Setup)**
1. Click the extension icon
2. Click **"Settings"** (⚙️ gear icon)
3. Fill in:
   - **SIEBEL ID**: Your unique ID (e.g., kristinamajasa)
   - **TEAM**: Your squad name (e.g., Squad Judith)
   - **Test Mode**: Enable for 1-minute timer (disable for 22-minute timer)
4. Click **"Save Settings"**

---

### **Step 2: Start Tracking When You Answer a Call**

**When you answer a customer call:**
1. Click the extension icon
2. Click the big **"▶️ Start Tracking This Call"** button
3. You'll see a live countdown timer

**That's it!** The extension is now tracking your call.

---

### **Step 3: Get Template When Timer Finishes**

**After 22 minutes (or 1 minute in test mode):**
1. You'll get a **notification**
2. Extension badge turns **red with "!"**
3. Click the extension icon
4. Fill in **ISSUE** and **REASON**
5. Enter **Transaction ID** manually (or click "Re-scan")
6. Click **"Copy to Clipboard"**
7. Paste into Teams!

---

## 🧪 Testing the Extension

**Want to test without waiting?**

1. Make sure Settings are configured
2. Click extension icon
3. Click **"Test Template Now"**
4. Template appears instantly!
5. Try filling it out and copying

---

## ⏱️ Test Mode vs Production Mode

### **Test Mode** (1 minute timer)
- ✅ Good for: Testing, training, learning the extension
- ✅ Timer: 1 minute
- ✅ Enable in Settings

### **Production Mode** (22 minute timer)
- ✅ Good for: Real calls
- ✅ Timer: 22 minutes
- ✅ Disable test mode in Settings

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### "No Active Call" even though I'm on a call?
➡️ **Solution**: The extension can't auto-detect calls yet. You MUST click **"Start Tracking This Call"** button manually when you answer a call.

### Template didn't appear after 22 minutes?
➡️ **Check**:
1. Did you click "Start Tracking This Call" when the call started?
2. Is test mode enabled? (If yes, timer is 1 minute, not 22)
3. Open browser console (F12) and look for `[Long Call Update]` logs

### Transaction ID not found?
➡️ **Solution**: Just enter it manually! Copy it from your 8x8 interface and paste it into the "Or enter manually" field.

---

## 📝 Template Fields

- **TRANSACTION ID**: Copy from 8x8 (auto-detected or manual)
- **SIEBEL ID**: From settings (auto-filled)
- **SKILLSET**: PS or STD (auto-filled from settings)
- **TEAM**: From settings (auto-filled)
- **ISSUE**: YOU fill this in (why the call was long)
- **REASON**: YOU fill this in (reason for extended duration)

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Keep test mode ON** while learning the extension
2. **Click "Start Tracking" immediately** when answering a call
3. **Don't close the browser** - the timer runs in background
4. **Check the countdown** by clicking the extension icon anytime
5. **Use "Test Template Now"** to practice filling it out
